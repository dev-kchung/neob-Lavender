What is Busy Bee Gardens?

Sponsored by neob, Niagara Essential Oils & Blends, Busy Bee Gardens is the world’s first greenhouse operation dedicated to the innovation and preservation of aromatic plants. There are on-site lavender field trials, distillation and oil extraction. Methods of extraction include using steam, cold press and polar trichome extraction. The Busy Bee Gardens plant preservation project’s entire existence is dedicated to the preservation, innovation and implementation of millions of aromatic plants. Now in year five, the plant preservation project includes 15,000 sq feet of greenhouse space, over 4000 lavender plants and it is the only facility to distill essential oils using glass to glass technology. During 2013, neob innovation included “neob botanical beverages,” three new rose varieties and hardy orange trees. We invite you to enjoy our aromatic crops.
